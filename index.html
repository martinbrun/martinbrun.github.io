hi! i am a phd candidate and predoctoral research fellow at the department of applied economics of the universitat autònoma de barcelona (uab). i am a member of ecineq, equalitas, seu, and aee.

before starting my phd I have worked with united nations' eclac, the world bank, and the international labour organization in various research projects on labor, inequality and trade, with a special focus on development in latin america, south asia, and africa.

my current interests refer to applied economics, in the intersection of individual's social preferences and political behavior.
