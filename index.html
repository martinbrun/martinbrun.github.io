I am a PhD candidate and Predoctoral Research Fellow at the Department of Applied Economics of the Universitat Autònoma de Barcelona (UAB). I am a member of ECINEQ, EQUALITAS, SEU, and AEE.

Before starting my PhD I have worked with United Nations' ECLAC, the World Bank, and the International Labour Organization in various research projects on labor, inequality and trade, with a special focus on development in Latin America and South Asia.

My current interests refer to Applied Economics, in the intersection of individual's social preferences and political behavior.
